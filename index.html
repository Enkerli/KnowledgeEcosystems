This prototype is designed as a layered research interface rather than a complete scholarly chronology.

Seeded dataset note: this version adds richer thematic overlays and perspective filters, including cultural movements, literary circles, salons, and schools of thought, but it is still a starter corpus. It is ready for denser regional timelines, citations, and more granular institutional lineages.

Historical axis with synchronized tracks

Bars show duration; clicking highlights linked events across tracks. Use perspective presets and thematic overlays to shift the story told by the same underlying timeline.

Years BCE are shown as negative values internally and labeled as BCE on the axis.
Try presets such as Women and gender, Aotearoa New Zealand, Vietnam, Knowledge hubs, or Cultural movements and circles. Theme filters also work well with terms like credentialism, dilettantism, research funding, art periodization, and literary circles.
